Brief History

Palestine has a very long history: it has been inhabited since ancient times and was part of many historical empires. Important events in the 20th century changed the political landscape.

During the 20th century there were major demographic and political changes. Some communities were displaced and others remained; this shaped present-day realities. This summary is short and simplified.

Today many local and international efforts aim at development and peaceful coexistence. Culture and heritage remain very important for the people.
